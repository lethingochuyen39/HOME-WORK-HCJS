Briards

By Ivy Duckectt

The briard , or erger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed Historry

The briard , which is believed to have riginated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hesding. Brards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction.

currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne,Napoleon,Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned briards.
